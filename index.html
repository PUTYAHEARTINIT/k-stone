About K Stone

K Stone is an Atlanta-based rapper and artist bringing authentic storytelling and raw energy to the hip-hop scene. With a unique sound and uncompromising vision, K Stone represents the next generation of Southern hip-hop.


From the streets of Atlanta to the studio, every track tells a story. Real music. Real message. No compromises.
