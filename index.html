The Story

Kameron Walker, known as K-Stone, is a multi-disciplinary artist from Watts, California — rapper, producer, songwriter, creative director, and actor.

From the streets of Watts to Carson, raised in a two-parent household alongside his brother Karon ("Beak"), athletics shaped his early years. Football. Track. Championships at the Coliseum. State Meet qualifications. But music was calling.

Instead of collegiate athletics, Kameron chose music and entertainment. His breakthrough came through Hearts of Stone, a collaborative group with his brother, producing the viral single "Work On You" featuring Davion Farris in 2013-2014.

He joined Non-Affiliates, opening for major artists. His 2016 solo project "Off The Blocc 1994" generated serious momentum. Then came "Hood B****" featuring Allie Kay — his real introduction as an established artist.

Now based in Atlanta, K-Stone continues to evolve across multiple creative disciplines, bringing authentic storytelling and raw energy to everything he touches.

"Peace to me is success. Self Awareness. Being Happy."
