Who is K-Stone

Built in Watts.
Raised in Carson.
Made in the Lab.

Kameron Walker — known professionally as K-Stone — is an independent West Coast artist whose identity is rooted in transformation, discipline, and cultural self-definition. Raised between the East Side of Watts and Del Amo in Carson, California, his early years were shaped by athletics and structured competition.

A standout two-sport athlete who reached the State Meet in track and played in a Championship football game at the Coliseum, K-Stone's transition from athlete to entertainer was never a pivot — it was an evolution. The same performance mindset that drove him on the field became the foundation of his artistry.

As a rapper, songwriter, producer, creative director, and emerging actor, K-Stone defines success on his own terms: "Peace. Self awareness. Being happy."

The Journey

THE EVOLUTION

94

Born in the Watts

Born and raised on the East Side of Watts off 103rd & Paloma Ave, before moving to Del Amo, Carson, CA at age 3. Two-parent household. One sibling: Karon "Beak" Walker.

Origin
09–13

The Athlete

Carson Colts football. Light Speed track club. Baseball and basketball at Mills, Anderson, Del Amo parks. Junior Year: Championship game at the Coliseum + State Meet in the 4×1. Two-sport competitor built on discipline, precision, and performance under pressure.

Athletics
15

Hearts of Stone

Collaborative work with PUTYAHEARTINIT yields the viral record "Work On You" — opening doors to live performance opportunities, including stages on The Game's Documentary 2 Tour. First taste of professional performance at scale.

Breakthrough
16

Going Solo

Releases Off The Blocc 1994 — his solo debut that establishes individual artistic voice and earns city-wide recognition. "Hood B***h" ft Allie Kay follows, cementing his introduction to the game as a real artist on his own terms.

Independence
26

KRA$H OUT

The latest chapter. KRA$H OUT (2026) demonstrates continued commitment to growth without reliance on industry gatekeepers — a fully independent release from an artist who has built something real, brick by brick, block by block.
